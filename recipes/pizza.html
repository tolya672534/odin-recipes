Once your pizzas are loaded with toppings, it's time to fire up the grill! Unless you have an outdoor pizza oven (see below), an outdoor grill is your best bet for backyard pizza. Using a pizza stone or steel is going to help you mimic that wood-fired taste. Baking pizza on aluminum foil will also work, but it won't produce quite the same crust.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Insert Pizza Stone and Preheat Grill
  2. Slide Pizza Onto Stone
  3. Grill
